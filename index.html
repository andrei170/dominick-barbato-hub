Compliance Notes (FTC ed-side / income-claims)

+

You are an educator running a vending-business mentorship, so the build stays carefully on the educator side and frames your own results as your story, not as a promise to participants. Everything we ship for you has these guardrails:

  • "Individual results vary" disclaimer on landing + FAQ + footer
  • Your self-reported numbers (12 Vendstars in 2015, 100+ machines placed, 18 companies started / 9 failed, 2x best-selling author) framed as your story, not as a projection of participant outcomes
  • No specific "make $X/mo" income guarantee anywhere
  • No fabricated student testimonials (only Abe's first-name consultation feedback exists in your public surface, and it's not used here since it's not a mentorship outcome)
  • Outcome language uses "first machine / first paying location / first restock cycle / first repeating route revenue" milestones, not dollar numbers
  • FAQ explicitly states no income guarantee, addresses capital, addresses non-NY operators, addresses day-job operators

This keeps you in the educator lane the FTC expects for business-opportunity mentorships, while still letting you sell the Accelerator and the method confidently.
